Franklin's yearly Family Festival was a wonderful event this year. The weather held out beautifully for the annual April event. The skys were a beautiful blue with occasional brilliant white clouds. Colorful and fresh produce was shipped in from the south and displayed in rainbows atop the tables to provide a beautiful and healthy feast for all families.
